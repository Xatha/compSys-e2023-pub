Big endian: Order by most significant bit first
Little endian: Order by least significant bit first

We have the address 0xABCDEF12. Assume that the first address is 0 and the word size is 4 bytes.


Big Endian:

Bytes | Word
0       AB
1       CD
2       EF
3       12

Little Endian:

Bytes | Word
0       11
1       EF
2       CD
3       AB
